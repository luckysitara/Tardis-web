The Financial OS

Hardware-Native Auth

Simultaneous check for physical hardware and the Seeker Genesis Token (SGT). Your device is your sovereign key.

Signed Social Layer

Every post and reaction is cryptographically signed by the Seed Vault. Verify identity, eliminate bots.

Zero-Knowledge DMs

Private 1-to-1 messaging with keys derived from hardware. Your conversations stay between you and the recipient.

P2P Lending & Borrowing

Access collateralized peer-to-peer loans directly within the app. Secure, transparent, and hardware-attested.

Tipping & Micropayments

Send SOL or SPL tokens instantly to any user in the Town Square or DMs with a single hardware-signed tap.

Social Commerce (Blinks)

Buy, sell, and trade products directly in your social feed using integrated Solana Actions and Blinks.

Pump.fun & Jupiter Launch

Launch new tokens on the bonding curve or execute deep liquidity swaps via Jupiter without leaving the app.

Gated Colonies
